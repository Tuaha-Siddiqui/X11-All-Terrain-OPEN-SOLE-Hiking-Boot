X11 Hiking Technologies is a research-driven outdoor gear company founded in 2025. Our team of engineers, biomechanics experts, and avid hikers came together to solve a critical challenge: how to improve natural foot function on the trail. The result is the X11 Open-Sole Hiking Boot, the first of its kind.

We conduct rigorous field testing and collaborate with sports scientists to refine our designs. Based in the Pacific Northwest, X11 operates a biomechanics lab and collaborates with athletic gait researchers. Our mission is to bring science-backed innovation to the hiking community, helping outdoor enthusiasts move more efficiently and safely.

For inquiries, collaborations, or research partnerships, contact us at info@x11-opensole.com.
